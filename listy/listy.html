MY SEVEN-LIFE PUFF-PUFF RECIPE

Chef May


Check the author's profile

Ingredients

  • Dry Ingredients
    • 2 cups of flour
      • white flour
      • brown flour
    • 1 cup of sugar
    • 1/4 cup of milk powder
    • 1/2 tablespoons of baking powder
    • a pinch of salt
    • 1/2 tablespoon ofnutmeg
    • 1 tablespoon of instant yeast
  • Wet Ingredients
    • 1 cup of warm water
    • 1/2 cup of evaporated milk
    • 2 scotch bonnet peppers finely chopped
    • 5 cups of vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Pour out your dry Ingredients and mix properly
  2. Pour out the warm water and mix till fluffy and thick consistency
  3. Pour in the chopped scotch bonnet peppers
  4. Add in the evaporated milk
  5. leave to rest for 40 minutes
  6. fry in boiling hot oil at 150oC
  7. strain into a perforated bowl and leave to rest for 10-15 minutes
  8. serve with tomato sauce and enjoy
